02 — Problem

Why Equity Research
Is Still Fragmented

Users currently juggle multiple disconnected tools — creating wasted time, cognitive overload, and a higher barrier for beginners.

  • 1
    Financial dashboards for numbers
  • 2
    News websites for context
  • 3
    Notes apps and spreadsheets for watchlists
  • 4
    Separate tools for interpretation
  • !
    The problem is not lack of information — it is lack of integration and context.
03 — Solution

Our Solution:
Stock & Tell

One search. One brief. Everything in one place.

Input
Search a company
User enters any company name or ticker to trigger the research pipeline.
Retrieval
Market & news data
Platform pulls live market/financial data alongside recent relevant news articles.
Output
Structured company brief
A clean, readable summary combining numbers with business context — not just raw data.
Persistence
Save, revisit, export
Save to collections and watchlists. Revisit previous analyses. Export as JSON or CSV.

Stock & Tell is an investment support tool — not an automated decision-maker.

06 — Core User Journey

How a User Uses
Stock & Tell

A four-step workflow designed to be frictionless from first search to saved insight.

STEP 01
Search
Enter a company name or ticker symbol into the search bar.
STEP 02
Receive
View a structured company brief combining market data and relevant news.
STEP 03
Save
Save the brief to a named collection. Group collections into watchlists.
STEP 04
Revisit
Return to saved analyses or rerun for fresh data. Cached retrieval improves speed.

09 — Interoperability

Designed for Reuse
Across the Ecosystem

Our services aren't tied to one frontend — they're built to be consumed by other teams and systems.

API Design
RESTful & documented
OpenAPI / Swagger documentation. JWT + API key authentication. Structured JSON responses.
Data Standards
ADAGE-compatible exports
Structured exports that feed dashboards, pipelines, or future ML systems. No data lock-in.
For Other Teams
Consumable outputs
Other teams can consume our collection service. Reduces duplicated data collection logic.
Why It Matters
Platform, not a silo
Reusable backend capability with loose coupling — the difference between a product and a platform.
